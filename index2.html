To Do List📝

Instructions:

1. Add new tasks by writing in the ✏️add a new task box and clicking the add button

(empty tasks won't work! if you don't type but click add, an alert ⚠️ will prompt you to input a task)

2. To delete a task, hover over the task and click on the bin emoji 🗑️

3. To mark a task as completed, hover over the task and click the check emoji ✅

(you can unstrike a completed task by re-clicking on the check emoji✅ )

Have fun and stay productive!
